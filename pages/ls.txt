
	
	ls
	
	Show you what's in a folder.
	More information: https://www.gnu.org/software/coreutils/manual/html_node/ls-invocation.html.


		- See files one per line:
		ls -1

		- See everything, including hidden files:
		ls --all

		- See the files by their types:
		ls --classify
	
		- See a detailed list of everything:
		ls -l --all
