

	rm
	
	Deletes files and folder. 
	More information: https://www.gnu.org/software/coreutils/manual/html_node/rm-invocation.html.
				[38;2;255;66;69m
	Warning: *This is permanent. There is no trash can.*
				[0m

		- Delete a file, asking for confirmation first. (This is the safest way):
		rm -I file.txt
		
		- Delete a file without asking. (less safer):
		rm file.txt

		- Delete a folder and everything inside it safely:
		rm -Ir folder/


	 Your folder is empty? See "rmdir" page to remove it safely.
