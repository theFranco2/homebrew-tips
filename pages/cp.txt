

	cp
	
	Copy files and folders.
	More information: https://www.gnu.org/software/coreutils/manual/html_node/cp-invocation.html.

		
		- Copy a file:
		cp file.txt copied.txt

		- Copy a file into another folder:
		cp file.txt path/to/folder

		- Copy a folder and everything inside it:
		cp -r folder/ path/to/destination/
